About me

I am a photographer born in Transylvania the "land beyond the forest" a Romanian living in beautiful and historic Norwich in the United Kingdom. I have a deep passion for digital photography and videography. I get my inspiration from outdoor activities, beautiful landscapes, travelling the world and exploring urban scenery. Over the years as a amateur photographer I have managed to improve my skills and in 2012 I won the Wanderlust travel photo competition: LINK HERE , with positive comments from the famous Steve Davey - travel photographer: LINK HERE. I hope you enjoy viewing my photos and videos as much as I did taking and creating them.
